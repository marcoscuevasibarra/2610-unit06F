History of Smartphone

Mobile phones were invented as early as
the 1940s when engineers
working at AT&T.

1926: The first mobile telephony service
was in the Deutsche Reichsbahn.

1982: Engineers and administrators from eleven
European countries gathered in Stockholm
to consider a European digital cellular
phone system.

1992: The world is first ever SMS message
was sent in the UK.

Landmark phones: The handsets that made history

1998: The first downloadable content sold to mobile phones

1999: Emojis were invented by Shigetaka Kurita in Japan.
